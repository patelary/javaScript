Stone, Paper and Scissor Game

How to play this game:

Step 1: In order to play this game, we need minimum two player. Here the game is between user and computer. You just have to give integer from 1 to 3 as 1 represents Rock, 2 represents Paper and 3 represents Scissor you must choose each time you play

Step 2: Remember rock is superior than scissor, scissor can cut paper, and paper can wrap the rock and throw it away.

Step 3: You can play this game as many times you want, or either you can exit and get result



Click the button to start the game
